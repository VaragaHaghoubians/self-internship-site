Project 2 — Retail Sales Performance Analysis

Role: Data Analyst Difficulty: Beginner–Intermediate Estimated Time: 6–9 hours

Problem Statement

You are a Data Analyst at a retail company that sells products across multiple categories, regions, and customer segments. The management team wants answers to key business questions before making decisions for the next fiscal year:

  • Which product categories and sub-categories are most profitable?
  • Which regions and states generate the most revenue?
  • How do sales and profit change over time? Are there seasonal trends?
  • Which customer segments are most valuable?
  • Are discounts actually helping or hurting profit?

Your job is to clean the data, answer these questions with aggregations and visualizations, and present your findings in a clear, structured format that a non-technical manager can understand.

Dataset

Name: Sample Superstore Sales Dataset Source: Kaggle Direct Link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final File: Sample - Superstore.csv Size: ~10,000 rows, 21 columns

How to Download:

  1. Go to the Kaggle link above
  2. Log in to your Kaggle account
  3. Click Download
  4. Rename the file to superstore.csv
  5. Place it inside the data/ folder in this project directory

Alternative Dataset (if Kaggle is unavailable):

  • UCI Online Retail Dataset: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/352/online+retail
  • This is a UK-based e-commerce dataset — very similar analysis applies

Key Columns:

ColumnDescription
Order DateDate the order was placed
Ship DateDate the order was shipped
SegmentCustomer segment: Consumer, Corporate, Home Office
RegionUS region: East, West, Central, South
CategoryProduct category: Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology
Sub-CategoryMore specific product type
SalesRevenue from the order item
QuantityNumber of units ordered
DiscountDiscount applied (0.0 to 1.0)

Business Questions This Project Answers

  1. What is the total revenue, total profit, and overall profit margin?
  2. Which product Category and Sub-Category generates the most profit?
  3. Which Region and State have the highest sales?
  4. Is there a seasonal pattern in sales over time?
  5. Which Customer Segment is most valuable?
  6. What is the relationship between Discount and Profit?
  7. What is the average order processing time (Order Date to Ship Date)?
  8. Which Sub-Categories are actually losing money (negative profit)?

Skills Demonstrated

  • Data loading and inspection
  • Date parsing and time series analysis
  • GroupBy aggregations with .groupby() and .agg()
  • Sorting, filtering, ranking
  • Creating KPI summaries
  • Multi-type visualization (bar, line, scatter, heatmap, map)
  • Identifying business insights and communicating them clearly

Expected Outputs

  1. 10+ visualizations saved in outputs/figures/
  2. A KPI summary table (revenue, profit, margin, top category, etc.)
  3. Answers to all 8 business questions in a clear report
  4. A recommendation section with 3–5 actionable suggestions
